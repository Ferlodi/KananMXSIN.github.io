A SHORT HISTORY

THIS PROJECT BECAME KNOWN WHEN OUR TEACHER MET WITH MY CLASSMATE JOSE CARLOS TO TALK ABOUT SOMETHING HE WAS CONCERNED ABOUT, THESE ARE THE CASES OF CHILD DEATH IN VEHICLES DUE TO HEATSTROKE. WHICH IN SHORTER WORDS MEANS THAT IT HAPPENS WHEN A CHILD IS LOCKED IN THE CAR FOR ANY REASON AND SUFFERS FROM THE HIGH TEMPERATURES THAT ARE GENERATED UNTIL HE LOSES HIS LIFE.

SO WE CAME UP WITH THE IDEA OF SOLVING IT WITH A PROJECT, SO WE GOT DOWN TO WORK AND BEGAN TO FOLLOW AN INVESTIGATION ON THE CASES THAT OCCURRED, HOW THEY OCCURRED, AND HOW THEY AFFECTED. WE WERE SURPRISED BY THE STATISTICS TO DISCOVER THAT THEY WERE VERY FREQUENT CASES, SO MUCH SO THAT WHEN WE INVESTIGATED AND QUESTIONED SOME OF MY FAMILY MEMBERS CONFESSED THAT I WAS A VICTIM OF THIS TYPE OF SITUATION, EVERYTHING SEEMS TO BE FINE AT THE TIME OF CREATING THIS WEBSITE.

ANOTHER THING THAT WAS UNVEILED FROM ALL THIS RESEARCH THAT MADE IT POSSIBLE TO FORMULATE A SOLUTION FOR THIS, WAS THAT THE ONLY STATISTICS THAT THERE WERE FOR THIS TYPE OF CASES QUANTIFIED THE DEATHS ONLY BUT IN NO OTHER TYPE OF DATA OR GRAPHS SHOWED THOSE AFFECTED BY SUFFERING THIS TYPE OF SITUATIONS, BECAUSE IF THEY SURVIVE THEY CAN SUFFER MANY MORE CONSEQUENCES AND WITH AN ACT AS SIMPLE AND APPARENTLY BANAL AS LEAVING YOUR CHILDREN IN A CONSCIOUS OR NEGLIGENT WAY FOR A MOMENT IN THE CAR. MANY OF US BELIEVE THAT SUCH A SITUATION COULD HAPPEN, BUT IT MAY BE MORE COMMON THAN YOU THINK.

WE CREATED A SOLUTION TO AVOID THIS TYPE OF SITUATION.

KANAN IS AN INNOVATION THAT WE HAVE CREATED AND DESIGNED FROM A SET OF SENSORS FORMING AN ELECTRONIC DEVICE THAT AIMS TO MONITOR INFANTS, IN ORDER TO PREVENT ACCIDENTS AND IF THEY OCCUR TO NOTIFY OR MONITOR, SO WE AVOID FATAL ACCIDENTS THAT COST US LIVES AND ALSO DAMAGES THEIR CLOSE CIRCLE FOREVER.
