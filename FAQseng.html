WHAT DOES KANAN MEAN?

KANAN COMES FROM THE MAYAN LANGUAGE, SPECIFICALLY FROM THE WORD "Kananpal" IS A PERSON WHO TAKES CARE OF CHILDREN. KANAN BY ITSELF IS DEFINED AS A GUARDIAN THAT REFERS TO OUR PROJECT.

WHAT IS KANAN? AS YOU COULD SEE IN THE BEGINNING KANAN IS AN ELECTRONIC DEVICE WITH WHICH YOU CAN TAKE IN YOUR CAR AND IN REAL TIME SHOW YOU CAR DATA, BESIDES BEING ABLE TO NOTIFY YOU IN CASE YOU FORGET YOUR CHILD IN THE CAR.

HOW DOES IT WORK??
KANAN WORKS BY MEANS OF AN ELECTRONIC DEVICE SUFFICIENTLY EQUIPPED FOR THE MONITORING OF MINORS IN THE VEHICLE, THIS IN A BRIEF EXPLANATION WORKS BY MEANS OF A SIM THAT SENDS DATA TO A SERVER AND THIS ONE RESPONDS BEING ABLE TO MONITOR THE INTERIOR OF THE CAR .
